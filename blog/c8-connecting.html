How did you find the loving kindness meditation?

I found the loving kindness meditation rather sad in contrary to the happiness overall feeling mentionned in the book Search Inside Yourself. I directed the exercise toward my mum who is living very far away from me. As a daughter, I always find it hard to see my parents in the lense of actual living human beings. To me, they are magical creatures. But obviously no... or not too much. I find that thinking of my mum and projecting a wish for her to be free from pain, not too get harmed or sad reminded me that she could feel that.
This made me profundly sad because she is far and I know she endures me being away but does not enjoy it. I felt like I was a reason for her to be sad and that in the same time there was very little I could do to avoid it without going back to her.
I think this exercise can bring happiness as a feeling, definitively when words are chosen less heavily (like if I use "wants to be happy" instead of "wants not to get sad") and with more focus on the other person fully. I think I let my personnal feelings and blame get in the way.
