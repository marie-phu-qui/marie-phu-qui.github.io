How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens ?

Search inside Yourself define the 'please, passion and purpose'lens as : " 1.Pleasure: This type of happiness is about always chasing the next high. It is the rock-star type of happiness because it is very hard to maintain unless you are living the lifestyle of a rock star. 2. Passion: Also known as “flow,” where peak performance meets peak engagement, and time flies by. 3. Higher Purpose: This is about being part of something bigger than yourself that has meaning to you."


This makes sense to me. I would be more prone to add a distraction phase instead of the 'pleasure' one. Indeed the rock-star high of pleasure matters, but this description makes it sounds as a sugar high to me. As a person I'll would have a tendency to push even passion related actions away if they feel too intense for my mood of the moment. I'll would lazy out and reach for an easy distraction (watching series or reading a whole book would do it), this kind of pleasure are easy to reach and do not need to meet a rock-star lifestyle. That is why I think tall the spectrum needs to be reflected in one action for it to be justifiable to hold onto for a lenght of time without guiltying on our life time spent.
